About

Welcome to the Knight's Academy

Ask any player which piece they fell in love with first, and most will say the knight. It jumps where nothing else can, threatens two pieces at once, and turns “impossible” positions into forced wins. Beginners light up the moment they spot their first fork. Grandmasters still smile at a well-placed outpost. This is where that love affair becomes a skill.

Chess strength is built from pattern recognition, not memorization. Every puzzle here isolates one knight idea — a fork, an outpost, a mating net like Anastasia's or the Arabian — so your brain can drill it until it's instinct. Short reps, tight feedback, real progress. That's deliberate practice, knight-style.

Train a little every day and you won't just get better at chess — you'll start seeing the whole board differently. Welcome to your knight era.
